Everyday Rights in Pakistan: Flashcards

What are your rights regarding **Protection from Arbitrary Arrest or Detention** in Pakistan?
  • Right to life and liberty.
  • Must be produced before a magistrate within **24 hours**.
  • Right to **legal counsel** during interrogation.
  • Physical or **custodial abuse is illegal** (Torture Act 2022).
  • Laws: Constitution (Article 10A), CrPC Sections 54, 61, 167.
What is your right to **Public Information Access** in Pakistan?
  • Right to **information from any government department**.
  • Request must be replied to within **10-30 days**.
  • Laws: RTI Act 2017, PPA + Punjab RTI Act.
What protections do tenants have against **Illegal Eviction** in Pakistan?
  • **No eviction without court order** or legal notice.
  • Verbal leases still have legal weight.
  • Law: Punjab Rented Premises Act.
What are your rights regarding **Fair Wages and Job Security** in Pakistan?
  • **Minimum wages** are protected.
  • **Overtime pay** for more than 8 hours/day.
  • **Severance pay** (30 days/year worked).
  • Women: **12-16 weeks paid maternity leave**.
  • Laws: Minimum Wages Ordinance, Industrial Relations Act, Provincial Employees Social Security Ordinance.
What are your **Digital & Privacy Rights** in Pakistan?
  • **No data leaks or harassment**.
  • No one can access your data/SMS/messages **without consent**.
  • **Cyber harassment** and impersonation are punishable by law.
  • **Unauthorized data access/modification/transmission** is punishable.
  • Laws: PECA 2016, Data Protection Bill 2023.
What are your rights when **Dealing with Police** in Pakistan?
  • **No arrest without reason**.
  • Right to know reason for arrest, warrant, FIR, or complaint.
  • Can ask for **full name** and **officer ID**.
  • Women can only be **searched/arrested by female officers**.
  • Laws: Police Order 2002, CrPC, Torture Act 2022.
What are your rights regarding **Free Legal Help** in Pakistan?
  • **Free legal aid** for eligible low-income citizens.
  • Covers civil, criminal, family, child, and women's rights cases.
  • Law: Legal Aid Authority Act 2020.
What are your **Consumer Rights** in Pakistan?
  • Right to **complain** and get **receipts**.
  • Shops must **display prices** and issue receipts.
  • Laws: Punjab Consumer Protection Act 2005, Sindh CPA 2014.
What are the rights related to **Children's Protection** in Pakistan?
  • **No child labor/marriage**.
  • **Child marriage** (under 18) is a crime.
  • Laws: Juvenile Justice Act 2018, Zainab Alert Act 2020.
